## <a name="preamble"></a> Preamble

I'm writing about this from about the halfway mark of my first novel, *Ang Paghihiwalay*. At this point, my protagonist is grappling with how he will respond to the allegations of impropriety levelled against the priest who occupied the post before he arrived in 1860s Philippines.

Stories about the Epstein files had been showing up on my feed more and more over the last year or so. But I was absolutely unprepared for what ended up being in those files.

The purported email invitation from Edward Jay Epstein (whom I'd never heard of) to Ghislaine Maxwell (whom I seem to learn more and more about every day) to sit on a **'Shadow Commission of 9/11'**, especially caught my attention.

On that basis, I opted to look very specifically into what else was in the files involving Edward Jay Epstein (eje below). The initial search turned up 1904 results, but that inluded 'ed' in any form (for example: 'op-ed'). It also included other Eds such as:
 - Ed Klein - working on a PR article for Vanity Fair  doing damage control in 2012 
 - Ed Boyden - visiting the ranch for a retreat titled 'Mathematics of Deception' in 2013
 - Ed Cuccia, and  others.

I'm hearing all of those names for the first time. I'm also very disconnected from all of this and have no way of `verifying` any of it. 

After going through all the emails, removing duplicates (except duplicates of `Shadow Commission` email) and `other Ed's`, I finally ended up 111 emails of interest (including some interesting sidenotes). Most of them are from 2012 and 2013.
This is made up of:
 - 56 emails from Edward
 - 17 emails to Edward
 - 7 emails mentioning Edward

This has all been compiled by me. I present some impressions but am making no attempt to accuse anyone of criminality. This article does discuss at least one actor who has been found guilty, convicted and pardoned.

My goal is just to present what I've found in these files and do my small part with shedding light on this massive filestore of global import and implication. The 9/11 claim especially, seems to me very underreported for such a serious allegation.

I hope this humble effort can do some small part in helping to support those who are pressing forward for truth and justice. I know many are facing threats, lawsuits and I can only imagine what else.

I've heard these emails referred to as `raw intel` and I think it's an apt description. The information here is unverified and many of the emails appear to have been stripped of their context.

If this document is released by the government, is it to be believed that Ed Epstein actually sent the `Shadow Commission` email? If so, what was he referring to? At the end of the day I don't know and won't try to speculate. For sure the intelligence agencies have the rest of the story. 

There are so many serious issues being brought to light here. I hope the guilty will be held to account, even if the handling so far makes such a possibility seem very bleak right now.

I'll also not that JMail, my main tool for searching, does not seem to include the [23,000+ files released in November 2025](https://journaliststudio.google.com/pinpoint/document-view?collection=2283eeed70befac7&p=1&docid=a295fa208b06acec_2283eeed70befac7_0&dapvm=2). Those files are [searchable here](https://thehill.com/homenews/house/5602294-epstein-documents-oversight-republicans-release/)
I can only pray that this and the many other scandals will help them find the strength to do the right thing. Better late than never...

Tuloy tuloy lang!

## <a name="section24"> Closing thoughts

That's an interesting place to leave this `saga`. It gives the impression that EJE and JEE will generate a story for JEE's defense similar to what they did with DSK.

I don't really believe that there is no further communication between the men after this. There are probably lots of emails still missing.

We start out with a 9/11 shadow commission and finish with JEE & EJE getting ready to discuss working on a book. Seems like a thoughtfully controlled release of information to me.

Clearly EJE's investigation and reporting were key to getting DSK off the hook (after losing the election to Sarkozy). No idea what really happened in the case though.

Seeing Leon Black, Mike Milken and Michael Wolff seem to show up consistently during the days that the two Epsteins meet is interesting. I don't see anything in here to suggest a crime being committed. Would future releases fill in the blanks?
